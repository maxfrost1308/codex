Step 1 — Print Calibration Template

This generates a PDF with alignment crosses at the top-left and bottom-right corners (10mm from edges), printed on both front and back. Print it duplex, at 100% scale (no fit-to-page), then measure the offset between front and back crosses.

Step 2 — Enter Measurements

How to measure: Hold the printed sheet up to light so you can see both front and back crosses overlaid. Measure how far the back cross has shifted relative to the front cross at each corner. The sign convention is printed on the template itself.
