Print at 100% scale — disable "fit to page" or you'll lose the bleed-to-edge effect. For duplex on a single sheet: short-edge binding for landscape pages, long-edge binding for portrait, so page ii lands right-side-up when bound. Source images below ~220 ppi at the chosen page size will print soft; the tool flags this in the source line.
